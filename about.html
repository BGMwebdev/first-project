About Me

My name is Marleen, 29 years old, daughter, sister of 2 brothers, partner of my dear boyfriend, yoga teacher, studying in family constellations and human design and have a love for expression, sunlight and water. I decided very intuitively to drastically change my life, puzzle pieces are increasingly falling into place and the longing for wholeness pushes me forward in this life, lovingly and sometimes painfully. The bit that touches me now is reconnecting with women, old wounds, which can only be healed there, where it was once torn, in connection to other women. To stand strong as a woman, I have to look at the woman, in all her facets. That is my motivation for a women's circle, not as an example, but mainly to surrender to something bigger than me.

My Mission

According to Hopi Prophecy, “When the women gather the world will heal.” This embodies the concept of the need for divine feminine energy in communal leadership, after centuries of patriarchal control. In order to shift global perspectives and interactions from a masculine competition based system to an egalitarian, communal, and nurturing system, we need divine feminine energy in our leadership roles. If we truly hope to make a lasting impact on making the world around us better, we must begin by bettering ourselves, and for women this can be facilitated by regularly communing with like minded women to help heal our wounds, build each other up, and collaborate on bettering our communities. I think deep down every woman longs for sisterhood, for women to support each other instead of competing with each other. Let me facilitate being better together!
